# Commerce Sources

E-commerce and marketplace trends indicating consumer behavior.

## Overview

Commerce sources track product trends, bestsellers, and purchasing patterns across major marketplaces. These sources reveal what consumers are actively buying and searching for.

## Sources

- **AmazonBestsellersSource**: Amazon bestselling products across categories
- **EtsyTrendingSource**: Trending handmade and vintage items
- **AppStoreTopChartsSource**: Top apps and games in app stores

## Purpose

Commerce sources help identify:
- Product trends and consumer demand
- Seasonal purchasing patterns
- Emerging product categories
- Market gaps and opportunities
- Consumer pain points
