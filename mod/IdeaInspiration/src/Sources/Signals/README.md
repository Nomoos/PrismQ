# Signals Sources

Early indicators and emerging trends across digital platforms.

## Overview

Signals sources capture the earliest indicators of emerging trends before they become mainstream. These are the leading indicators that help predict what content will resonate.

## Subcategories

### Trends
Search and query trends that indicate rising public interest.

### Hashtags
Hashtag usage patterns that reveal trending topics and conversations.

### Memes
Viral meme formats and their spread across platforms.

### Challenges
Social media challenges that drive participation and engagement.

### Sounds
Trending audio content used in short-form videos.

### Locations
Geographic and location-based trending topics.

### News
Breaking news and current events driving conversations.

## Purpose

Signals sources help identify:
- Emerging trends before peak
- Rising topics and themes
- Viral momentum indicators
- Geographic trend patterns
- Cultural shifts and movements
