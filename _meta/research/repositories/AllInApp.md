## Overview

Generates podcast episodes from the "All-In" podcast using AI with complete audio workflow

## Key Features

- **Audio Transcription** - Whisper.cpp integration
- **Lesson Extraction** - spaCy NLP processing
- **Script Generation** - GPT-Neo LLM
- **Voice Cloning** - Coqui TTS
- **Show Art Creation** - Stable Diffusion
- **RSS Feed Generation** - Podcast distribution

## Technology Stack

- **Primary Language:** Python
- **Transcription:** Whisper.cpp
- **NLP:** spaCy
- **LLM:** GPT-Neo
- **TTS:** Coqui TTS
- **Images:** Stable Diffusion

## Comparison to PrismQ

### Strengths
- ✅ **Complete podcast workflow** - End-to-end audio production
- ✅ **TTS integration** - Voice synthesis
- ✅ **RSS feed** - Distribution capability

### Limitations
- ❌ **Podcast-specific** - Not multi-format
- ❌ **No video component** - Audio only
