## Overview

Automated video storytelling pipeline that turns online articles into narrated clips with custom scripts, titles, and visuals

## Key Features

- **Web Scraping** - Automated content sourcing
- **Summarization** - Article condensation
- **Custom Script Generation** - Narrative creation
- **TTS Narration** - Voice synthesis
- **Video Generation** - MoviePy integration
- **Tech News Focus** - Ideal for news recaps

## Technology Stack

- **Primary Language:** HTML/Python
- **Topics:** api, automation, llm, moviepy, pil, prompt-engineering, ranking, top-k, tts, video-synthesis, webscraping

## Comparison to PrismQ

### Strengths
- ✅ **Complete pipeline** - Article → Script → Audio → Video
- ✅ **TTS and video generation** - Multimedia creation
- ✅ **Automation-focused** - End-to-end process

### Limitations
- ❌ **Input is existing articles** - Not original content
- ❌ **No publishing or analytics** - Missing distribution
- ❌ **Limited to news/article format** - Narrow scope

## Extractable Mechanics

1. **Story-Driven Content Creation** - Narrative arc structure
2. **Web Scraping Automation** - Content sourcing
3. **Automated B-roll Selection** - Visual asset selection

## Implementation Priority

**Medium** - Story-driven approach (Phase 2) and web scraping patterns are valuable.
