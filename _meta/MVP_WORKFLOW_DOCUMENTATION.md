## Key Features

### Co-Dependent Improvement
Title and script improvements are interdependent - each is reviewed and refined based on the other, creating a continuous quality improvement cycle.

### 26-Stage Process
- **Stages 1-11**: Initial creation and refinement
- **Stages 12-13**: Acceptance gates
- **Stages 14-20**: Quality reviews (5 dimensions)
- **Stages 21-23**: Expert review and publishing

### Version Tracking
Progressive refinement through multiple versions (v1, v2, v3, v4+) with explicit acceptance gates.
