### 1. **Hook** (0:00-0:12)
> "The day after our wedding, she gave me a list of rules..."

- **Technique**: Cold open with immediate conflict
- **Purpose**: Grabs attention with shocking premise
- **Strength**: Specific, relatable, creates immediate questions

### 2. **Escalation / Setup** (0:12-0:40)
> Wife presents 11 rules including financial control, visiting restrictions, separate bedrooms, and written acknowledgment of her as "head of household"

- **Technique**: Stacking injustices
- **Purpose**: Build audience sympathy for narrator
- **Specific Rules Mentioned**:
  1. No visiting family without permission
  2. All money through her account
  3. Separate bedrooms during "emotional cycles"
  4. Written acknowledgment of her as head of household

### 3. **False Calm / The Twist Setup** (0:40-1:00)
> "I didn't argue. I just closed the folder and said, 'Okay, I'll go run some errands.'"

- **Technique**: Subverted expectations
- **Purpose**: Signal to audience that narrator has a plan
- **Strength**: Creates anticipation - "he's too calm"

### 4. **Revelation / Key Information** (1:00-1:40)
> Reveals the prenup with special clause about control/manipulation and $380,000 gift from father-in-law

- **Technique**: Information reveal
- **Purpose**: Give audience the "weapon" before the climax
- **Key Details**:
  - $380,000 down payment from father-in-law
  - Prenup clause: Any control/manipulation triggers repayment
  - Wife's "rules" document = dated evidence

### 5. **Rising Action** (1:40-2:20)
> Narrator contacts lawyer, acts normal for a week, wife brags to friends about "retraining" him

- **Technique**: Dramatic irony (audience knows what wife doesn't)
- **Purpose**: Build tension before payoff
- **Emotional Journey**: Satisfaction/anticipation for audience

### 6. **Climax / Payoff** (2:20-2:50)
> Legal paperwork arrives, wife opens it at party, father owes $412,000 within 30 days

- **Technique**: Public humiliation / justice served
- **Purpose**: Maximum emotional payoff

  - $412,000 total owed (original + interest)

### 7. **Father's Confrontation** (2:50-3:20)

- **Emotional Beat**: "Do you realize what you just cost this family?"

  - Father being audited
  - Public humiliation

### 8. **Resolution** (3:20-3:45)
> Wife loses house, social standing; narrator starts over

- **Technique**: Full consequences enumerated
- **Outcomes**:
